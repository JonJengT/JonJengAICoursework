In this unit, we went over adversarial searches, meaning searches when there are two players on opposing  teams. 
With computers, we can have Humans vs AI or AI vs AI.
We have decided to implement AI to tic tac toe using the MiniMax algorithm.
Basically, the computer searches through all possible options, and selects the best move to win the game with three in a row.

The tictactoe.py file was provided to me by my professor

to play against another human, or non-ai player you can run the following while in the ticTacToe folder:

python3 driver.py -o human -x human

In order to play against the ai:

python3 driver.py -o human -x ai

In order for the ai to play agianst the ai:

python3 driver.py -o ai -x ai

Since this game is complete, the AI is able to win or tie every single game.
